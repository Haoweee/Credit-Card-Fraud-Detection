Transactions Analysis

Credit Card Fraud Transaction datasets usually have a large class imbalance. To counter this large class imbalance I took all of the fraudulent cases and randomly sampled the same amount to balance the classes.

This shows the states that have the most fraudulent transactions.

This is a map of the United States, with the number of fraudulent transactions in each state. As you can see, the states where the population is greater there are more fraudulent transactions.

The top 5 most common categories that have fraudulent activity is grocery pos, shopping net, misc net, shopping pos, and gas transport.

Despite the top 5 most common categories that have fraudulent activity, different categories have different average costs. With shopping net being the highest, then shopping pos, and misc net

The average age of fraudulent customers peak twice. First the age of fraudulent customers grows and it peaks at 25-35 and at 45-55. Then there is a gradual decrease as the age gets older.

There is no significant difference in fraud male and female customers.
